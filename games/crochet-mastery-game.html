Interactive Crochet Mastery Game

Build your crochet skills through interactive choices and challenges

Welcome to the Crochet Mastery Game!

In this interactive 2D/3D game, you'll choose patterns, select yarns, and create beautiful crochet projects step by step. Each choice affects your final creation. Earn badges as you master different techniques!

Step 1: Choose Your Project

Let's start your crochet journey! Choose the project you'd like to make. Each project has different difficulty levels and will teach you different techniques.

Select one of the options below to begin:

Cozy Scarf

Difficulty: Beginner

Learn: Basic stitches

Beanie Hat

Difficulty: Intermediate

Learn: Working in rounds

Cute Amigurumi

Difficulty: Advanced

Learn: 3D shaping

Step 2: Choose Your Yarn

Great choice! Now let's select the perfect yarn for your project. The type of yarn affects both the appearance and feel of your finished piece.

Select one of these yarns:

Acrylic Yarn

Pros: Affordable, washable

Cons: Less breathable

Cotton Yarn

Pros: Soft, breathable

Cons: Heavier, less stretchy

Wool Yarn

Pros: Warm, elastic

Cons: Requires special care

Step 3: Choose Your Hook

Now let's select the right hook size for your project. The hook size affects the tightness and appearance of your stitches.

Based on your yarn choice, select one of these hooks:

Small Hook (3-4mm)

Creates tight, dense fabric

Good for: Amigurumi, bags

Medium Hook (5-6mm)

Creates balanced fabric

Good for: Scarves, hats

Large Hook (7-9mm)

Creates loose, drapey fabric

Good for: Quick blankets

Step 4: Choose Your Stitch Pattern

Now for the fun part - choosing the stitch pattern that will create the texture and look of your project.

Select one of these stitch patterns:

Single Crochet

Difficulty: Easy

Creates: Dense, firm fabric

Double Crochet

Difficulty: Easy

Creates: Tall, airy fabric

Shell Stitch

Difficulty: Intermediate

Creates: Decorative fabric

Step 5: Working on Your Project

Now let's put everything together and work on your project! Follow the instructions below.

Your custom instructions will appear here based on your choices.
